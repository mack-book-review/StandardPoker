

The following factors influence the decision to fold, call, or raise, as well as the amount to raise.
    (1)  Amount of money in the pot:  players who low on funds to play more conservatively and are therefore more
    likely to fold for large bets made by the other players.  All else being equal, they are also more likely to call rather than raise for smaller bets
    made by the other player.
    (2)  Quality of the hand:  for a high quality hand
    (3) Willingness to bluff
    (4) Amount of money bet by the other player
